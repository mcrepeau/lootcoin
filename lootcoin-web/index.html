The Lottery Pot

99% of all coins start in the pot

At genesis, 99,000,000 coins are locked in the lottery pot and 1,000,000 are in circulation. Miners compete to draw from it every block. Transaction fees continuously replenish it, keeping the lottery alive indefinitely.

How It Works

From block to payout

Mining in Lootcoin pays you in two ways: a guaranteed coin for every block, and a lottery ticket whenever your block includes transactions.

01
Include transactions to earn a lottery ticket
If your block includes at least one transaction, the node issues a loot ticket tied to your miner address and the block height. The payout scales with how many transactions were in your block: a block with at least 20 transactions earns the base payout; fewer transactions scales it down proportionally
02
Mine a block
Find a CubeHash-256 hash that meets the current difficulty target. Earn 1 lootcoin upon block acceptance — regardless of whether the block contains transactions.
03
Wait for maturity
Your ticket matures after 100 blocks, then settles within a 10-block reveal window — payout lands at block H+110 at the latest.
04
Payout lands in your wallet
One of 1,000,000 outcome buckets is drawn and results in one of four payout tiers — small wins are common, jackpots are rare. Every payout is a fraction of the current pot so the lottery never fully drains.

Fee Mechanics

Fees determine how fast you get included

There is no fixed fee schedule. When the network is at capacity, the higher your fee the sooner your transaction is eligible for inclusion.
