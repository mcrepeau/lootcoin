The Lottery Pot

99% of all coins start in the pot

At genesis, 99,000,000 coins are locked in the lottery pot and 1,000,000 are in circulation. Miners compete to draw from it every block. Transaction fees continuously replenish it, keeping the lottery alive indefinitely.

How It Works

From block to payout

01
Mine a block
Find a CubeHash-256 hash that meets the current difficulty target. Earn 1 lootcoin upon block acceptance — regardless of whether the block contains transactions. Half of all transaction fees in your block are paid to you immediately.
02
Include transactions to earn a lottery ticket
If your block includes at least one transaction, the node issues a loot ticket tied to your miner address and the block height. Payout is a flat fraction of the pot — the same whether your block has 1 transaction or 240.
03
Wait for the reveal window
Your ticket settles at block H+100, using the hashes of all 100 blocks from H to H+99 as randomness. The 100-block window is both the maturity delay and the entropy source — an attacker would need to control all 100 consecutive blocks to influence the outcome.
04
Payout lands in your wallet
One of 1,000,000 outcome buckets is drawn and results in one of four payout tiers — small wins are common, jackpots are rare. Every payout is a fraction of the current pot so the lottery never fully drains.

Fee Mechanics

Fees determine how fast you get included

There is no fixed fee schedule. The minimum fee is 2 coins — half goes directly to the miner, half to the lottery pot. When the network is at capacity, a higher fee gets you included sooner.
